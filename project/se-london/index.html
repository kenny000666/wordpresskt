Feeling At Home

With the birth of home working becoming the national norms in 2020, spaces are hungry for added purpose.

Metropolis living doesn't necessarily lend itself to suit a combination of work and sleep, however - I strongly believe that thoughtful design with gentle touches can bring life to spaces that are suitable for both.
Living Space
PROJECT GOALS

My client here was keen to revamp their existing spaces with soft tones and warm touches throughout their home to bring calm to the storm of the pandemic.

Dining Room
PROJECT GOALS

Attached to the living room is the dining area which threads these two areas together.

The family wanted to celebrate their closeness by having as little obstacle between each space as possible with a double door leading into the kitchen to conceal the smell whilst still feeling connected.

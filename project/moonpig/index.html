ABOUT PROJECT

The Heart of the Day

Designed as a playful townhall and social hub, this space brings people together in both spirit and purpose.

We envisioned a vibrant yet functional setting—one that seamlessly blends a teapoint with flexible, café-style working areas.

More than just a place to gather, it’s a space that energizes the everyday, sparking spontaneous conversations, creative thinking, and moments of pause.

By blurring the lines between work and connection, it invites a new rhythm into the day—one where ideas flow as freely as the coffee.
PROJECT GOALS

A space where the everyday is reimagined— where coffee brews alongside ideas, and connection becomes part of the workflow.

Designed to gather, to spark, to soften the edges of the workday with warmth, rhythm, and quiet joy.

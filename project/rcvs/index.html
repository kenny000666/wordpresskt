Collaboration BW Interiors
We were delighted to partner with the compassionate RCVS to design their new commercial office space. Our goal was to create an environment that reflects their mission of care, compassion, and community while providing a functional and inspiring workspace for their team.
ABOUT PROJECT

A Timeless Blend of Heritage and Modernity

The office design thoughtfully preserves much of the existing materiality, highlighting features like the exposed brickwork and traditional architectural details.

We intentionally retained these elements to guide the project’s character and direction. The overall aesthetic strikes a balance between modern classic and contemporary influences, creating a timeless yet fresh atmosphere.

Our aim was to craft a comforting, relaxed, and enduring space that feels both familiar and inspiring for our client.
ABOUT PROJECT

Breaking Down Barriers

Working with a multi-story office is never easy.

The key is not to break down social boundaries across the team and in turn - treat every team as part of the bigger family.

With careful planning and gentle integration across the entire floor plates, our ambitions were to tie everyone together seamlessly whilst respecting each individuals need for privacy, focus and social spaces.

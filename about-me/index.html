    BRIEF BACKGROUND

    As an interior designer, with a proven track record in both commercial and residential projects.

    My vast range of experiences has given me the confidence to take on any project from start to finish. My forte is pitch design, but my knowledge extends to concept design, FF&E, 3D modelling, technical drawings, and site coordination.

    I have honed my skills in AutoCAD, Revit & Enscape, Sketchup, Cinema 4D, Adobe Suite, and more to boost my efficiency, speed and understanding of each projects.

    It's an honour to have been nominated for the Class of 2019 Mix Magazine 30 Under 30s Award, and I'm confident that this is only the beginning.

    I am always thrilled about the opportunities in front of me, where I can use my skills to exceed client expectations, deliver beautiful projects, and create unforgettable spaces.
